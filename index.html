Cost for truck shipping

The cost to transport a truck, unlike other products and services, does not have a set dollar amount. Because no two truck transports are the same, our shipping experts will gather all details specific to your shipment and use the following variables to determine an accurate truck transport cost.

  • Truck Type – Truck’s year, make and model
  • Truck Dimensions – Length, height, width and weight of the truck. Dimensions must be verified.
  • Condition of Truck – Is the truck in operable condition? Please note shipping non-running vehicles are subject to additional fees.
  • Attachments – Are there any attachments on the truck? E.g. commercial trucks usually have a roof rack/mount, tool box, boom lift, trailer, water barrel etc.
  • Route – How far will truck be traveling? Where is truck picking up and delivering? Is the truck shipping to Hawaii?
  • Schedule – Are we working with a schedule? How early or late can we pickup and deliver?
  • Pickup/Delivery Location – Although our service is door to door we’ll need enough ample space at pickup and delivery for our driver to safely load and unload truck. Keep in mind our carriers are generally 80 feet in length.
