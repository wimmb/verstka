Cost for truck shipping

The cost to transport a truck, unlike other products and services, does not have a set dollar amount. Because no two truck transports are the same, our shipping experts will gather all details specific to your shipment and use the following variables to determine an accurate truck transport cost.

  • Truck Type – Truck’s year, make and model
  • Truck Dimensions – Length, height, width and weight of the truck. Dimensions must be verified.
  • Condition of Truck – Is the truck in operable condition? Please note shipping non-running vehicles are subject to additional fees.
  • Attachments – Are there any attachments on the truck? E.g. commercial trucks usually have a roof rack/mount, tool box, boom lift, trailer, water barrel etc.
  • Route – How far will truck be traveling? Where is truck picking up and delivering? Is the truck shipping to Hawaii?
  • Schedule – Are we working with a schedule? How early or late can we pickup and deliver?
  • Pickup/Delivery Location – Although our service is door to door we’ll need enough ample space at pickup and delivery for our driver to safely load and unload truck. Keep in mind our carriers are generally 80 feet in length.

FAQ

Do you work with dealerships?

Yes, we work with dealerships on a regular basis and offer unique conditions tailored to any type of dealership – whether it’s private or commercial.

Can you transport commercial equipment or utility vehicles between states?

Absolutely. We cooperate with various commercial and contractor companies to deliver equipment to project sites, emergency zones, or temporary work locations. Once the work is complete, we can transport the equipment back to the base.

Do you also work with private customers?

We specialize not only in dealership and commercial loads but also in private vehicle transport. Whether it’s from an auction or a private seller, we can handle the shipment safely and efficiently.

How is the price calculated?

Pricing is determined by several factors: whether the vehicle is operable or not, its type, size, and weight, the distance of transportation, and the urgency of delivery. We always provide a transparent quote before scheduling pickup.

Do you offer nationwide service?

Yes, we provide transportation services across all states in the U.S. No matter where your vehicle or equipment is located, we can arrange safe and timely delivery nationwide.
